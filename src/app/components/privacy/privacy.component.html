Privacy policy

When you connect to the online mode, SuperCodingBall stores your nickname and a link to your profile picture if you were connected with Facebook or Google.
To allow online players to play against each other, SuperCodingBall stores the blocks defining your team's strategy. To compute the players ranking, SuperCodingBall also stores the results of the games you played.
At any moment, you can choose to delete all this information by going to the online page, clicking your profile on the top right corner and choose "Delete my account".
SuperCodingBall uses Firebase Analytics to get anonymous statistics about the usage of the application.
